Las Vegas

Las Vegas is the Jewel of the Desert in Nevada. It is home to many, and has the prettiest sunsets I have ever seen in my life! Though many see Las Vegas as home to the many casinos, Las Vegas has so much more to offer. The population grows and brings in more ideas and forms of entertainment. Vegas homes both the Vegas Golden Knights for the NHL and the Las Vegas Raiders for the NFL. Lake mead is to the east of Las Vegas and supplies the valley with its water that is pumped from the famous Hoover Dam. Did you know that Las Vegas has a strong history with the mob? You can learn all about that in the Mob Museum downtown!
